Capstone Project

My last 4 months at Full Sail was spent developing 'Big Greasy'

Big Greasy is a cooking simulator game where you play as a fry cook at a fast food restaurant with a twist. The restaurant is haunted!

I was responsible primarily for antagonist systems such as the ghost that is haunting the main level.

I also made the rat AI. Rats spawn from a crack in the wall when a mess is made in the restaurant. Rats will target and eat food and trash on the floor. When a rat eats something another rat will spawn.

I programmed and contributed to several game objects such as doors, lights, trash, a teddy bear for the player to cry at to relieve fear etc. I even added cosmetic functionality to sinks and the shower in the player's apartment.

I designed and made a tutorial level to help players understand the fairly niche mechanics of the game. In order to ensure the best possible result I brought on friends to play-test our game.

I also took on responsibility to create audio and visual assets as needed.

I used a sounds proofed box to make original sound effects like doors opening and closing or scratching noises for the rats.

I used my Photoshop experience to quickly make a ice graphic that shows on the edge of the screen when the ghost is near.

This project is by far the most comprehensive and complete project I've done yet.

This is the trailer for the game on YouTube.
